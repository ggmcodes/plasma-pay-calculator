Quick Money FAQ

How fast can I get money?
You can have money in your pocket within 2-3 days. Same day if you're already a qualified donor at a center.
Do I really get paid the same day?
Yes! Money is loaded to a prepaid debit card immediately after your donation. You can use it right away or transfer to your bank.
What if I get rejected?
Most rejections are temporary (low iron, dehydration, recent illness). The center will tell you exactly what to fix to qualify next time.
Is plasma donation safe?
Yes, plasma donation is FDA-regulated and very safe. Uses sterile, single-use equipment. Millions of people donate regularly.
How much can I realistically make?
$50-120 your first week, $200-600 per month with regular donations. Varies by location and center.

⚠️ Important: Avoid Quick Money Scams

Plasma donation is legitimate because:

  • ✅ You never pay money upfront
  • ✅ Centers are FDA-regulated facilities
  • ✅ Payment is immediate and guaranteed
  • ✅ You're providing a valuable medical product

Avoid: "Make $500/day" schemes, anything requiring upfront payment, or "too good to be true" offers.
