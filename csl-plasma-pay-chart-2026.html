CSL Plasma Pay FAQ

How much does CSL Plasma pay in June 2026?

CSL Plasma pays $50-$120 per visit in June 2026, depending on your weight. Lighter donors (110-149 lbs) earn $50-$75, mid-weight donors (150-174 lbs) earn $55-$90, and heavier donors (175+ lbs) earn $65-$120. The second donation of each week pays more.

What is CSL's new donor bonus for 2026?

CSL Plasma's new donor program pays $1,000-$1,100 for your first 8 donations completed within 45 days. First donation typically pays $125-$150, with the 8th donation including a $175-$200 completion bonus.

Does CSL pay more for heavier donors?

Yes, CSL pays 30-50% more for donors over 175 lbs because the FDA allows collection of more plasma from heavier individuals. A 175+ lb donor can earn up to $120 per visit compared to $75 for a lighter donor.

How long does a CSL Plasma donation take?

First visit takes 2-3 hours (includes medical screening). Return visits take 45-90 minutes depending on your weight - heavier donors take longer because more plasma is collected.
