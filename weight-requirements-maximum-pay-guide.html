Why Weight Matters

Plasma centers follow FDA guidelines that limit plasma collection based on donor weight for safety reasons:

Plasma Volume by Weight Category:

110-149 lbs: 625-750 ml
150-174 lbs: 750-825 ml
175-199 lbs: 825-880 ml

FDA Safety Guidelines

The FDA sets these limits to ensure donor safety:

  • Total blood volume calculation: Based on height, weight, and gender
  • 12% rule: Can collect maximum 12% of total blood volume
  • Protein levels: Must maintain adequate protein levels
  • Recovery time: Heavier donors recover plasma volume faster

Dangerous Practices to Avoid
