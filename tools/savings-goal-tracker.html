Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I realistically earn from plasma donation? +
Most donors earn $50-$100 per donation, with 2 donations per week allowed. That's $400-$800 per month. New donors often earn more due to bonus programs - up to $1,000+ in the first month at some centers.
Is my data saved securely? +
Yes! Your donation data is saved locally in your browser using localStorage. We never send your personal donation information to any server. Your data stays on your device and is only accessible to you.
How often can I donate plasma? +
FDA regulations allow up to 2 plasma donations within a 7-day period, with at least 48 hours between donations. Most donors stick to a twice-weekly schedule (like Monday and Thursday) for maximum earnings.
What's the best way to reach my savings goal faster? +
Maximize earnings by: (1) Donating consistently twice per week, (2) Taking advantage of new donor bonuses at multiple centers, (3) Referring friends for referral bonuses, (4) Watching for promotional bonus periods, and (5) Maintaining good health to avoid deferrals.
Do I need to report plasma income on taxes? +
Technically, yes. Plasma compensation is considered taxable income by the IRS. However, most centers don't issue 1099 forms unless you earn over $600. We recommend consulting a tax professional and keeping records of your donations.
