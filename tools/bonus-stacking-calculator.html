Bonus Stacking FAQ

Is it legal to donate at multiple plasma centers? +
Yes, it's legal to donate at multiple centers, but you cannot donate at more than one center simultaneously. Most centers require you to complete their new donor program (usually 8 donations) before you can switch. There's also a national database that tracks donations to prevent over-donating.
How long do I have to wait between switching centers? +
This varies by center. Some require 56 days (8 weeks) since your last donation at another center. Others may have shorter or longer requirements. Always check with the new center before showing up, as policies change frequently.
Can I really earn $1,500+ using bonus stacking? +
Yes, if you have access to multiple centers and time your donations strategically. For example: $1,100 at BPL Plasma (first month) + $900 at CSL Plasma (second month) = $2,000 in two months. The key is completing each center's new donor program before moving to the next.
What's the best order to visit plasma centers? +
Generally, start with the center offering the highest new donor bonus and work your way down. Our calculator automatically sorts centers by their total bonus value to show you the optimal order.
Do bonus amounts change frequently? +
Yes, plasma centers regularly update their bonus offers based on demand. We update our calculator with the latest rates, but always verify directly with the center before planning your strategy. Sign up for center apps and emails to get notified of special promotions.
