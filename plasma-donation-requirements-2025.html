Eligibility Questions

What are the requirements to donate plasma?

To donate plasma, you must be 18-65 years old, weigh at least 110 pounds, pass a medical screening, and have valid ID. You must also pass protein and iron tests before each donation.

Can I donate plasma if I take medications?

Most medications are acceptable for plasma donation. However, blood thinners, certain antibiotics, and some other medications may temporarily disqualify you. Check with the plasma center about your specific medications.

How much do you need to weigh to donate plasma?

You must weigh at least 110 pounds to donate plasma. Higher weight donors (175+ pounds) can donate more plasma and earn more money per donation.
