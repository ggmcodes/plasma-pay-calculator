Frequently Asked Questions

Which states have the highest plasma donation rates?

California, Texas, Florida, and New York typically offer the highest plasma rates, with major cities paying $70-120 per visit. High cost-of-living areas generally offer better compensation to attract donors.

Do plasma rates vary significantly between states?

Yes, plasma rates can vary $20-40 per visit between states. Urban areas in expensive states like California and New York pay $80-120, while rural areas in lower cost states may pay $40-70 per visit.

Which states have the most plasma centers?

Texas, California, Florida, and Ohio have the most plasma centers nationwide. Texas leads with 200+ locations, followed by California with 150+ centers across major metropolitan areas.
