Common Questions

Everything you need to know about plasma donation earnings.

How much can I actually earn?

New donors can earn $50-120 per visit, with first-month bonuses reaching $900-1,200. Regular donors typically earn $400-700 monthly by donating twice per week.

Which centers pay the most?

CSL Plasma, BioLife, and Octapharma typically offer the highest rates. CSL averages $60-100 per visit, BioLife offers $50-90.

How often can I donate?

FDA regulations allow plasma donation twice per week with at least 48 hours between visits. This means up to 8-9 donations per month.

Is plasma income taxable?

Yes, plasma donation income is taxable. Centers issue 1099 forms for earnings over $600 annually. We recommend setting aside 20% for taxes.
