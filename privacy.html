Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information while you calculate plasma donation earnings.

Last Updated: January 20, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Important: No Personal Data Storage

Our plasma income calculator and earning estimator do not store any personal information. All earnings calculations are performed locally in your browser and are not transmitted to our servers.

Information We May Collect:

  • Website Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, browser type, and device information through analytics
  • Contact Information: Email address and message content when you contact us voluntarily
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser settings, and referring website

Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • Personal information from our calculator (weight, location, earnings estimates)
  • Eligibility quiz responses
  • Actual earnings or payment information
  • Financial account information
  • Social security numbers or government IDs

2. How We Use Information

We use the limited information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Calculator Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve our earnings calculator accuracy
  • Communication: Responding to your questions about plasma income calculations
  • Technical Maintenance: Monitoring website performance and security
  • Content Updates: Understanding which earning resources are most helpful to visitors

We do not use your information for marketing purposes or share it with third parties for their marketing use.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Essential Cookies

We use essential cookies that are necessary for the website to function properly, including:

  • Session cookies for calculator functionality
  • Security cookies to protect against malicious activity

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This helps us improve our resources and calculator. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using browser settings or Google's opt-out tools.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic and usage patterns. Google Analytics may collect information about your device and browsing behavior. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

External Links

Our website contains links to plasma centers and other external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Plasma Center Pay Rates

We display publicly available information about plasma center pay rates and earning opportunities. This information is gathered from public sources and center websites to help calculate your potential income.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect the limited information we collect:

  • Secure server infrastructure with SSL encryption
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited access to any collected information
  • Local processing of all earnings calculator data

While we strive to protect your information, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage users not to include sensitive information in contact messages.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 18 years of age. Plasma donation requires participants to be at least 18 years old to earn income. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

7. Your Rights

Regarding any personal information we may have:

  • Access: You can request information about what data we have collected
  • Correction: You can request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your information
  • Communication: You can opt out of communications from us

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@plasmapaycalculator.com.

8. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post any changes on this page and update the "Last Updated" date.

For significant changes, we may provide additional notice through our website or other communication methods.

10. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@plasmapaycalculator.com

Response Time: We respond to privacy inquiries within 7 business days

For general questions about plasma donation earnings or our calculator, use info@plasmapaycalculator.com.

Summary

We respect your privacy and collect minimal information. Our earnings calculator processes all data locally in your browser. We use analytics to improve our website and only collect contact information when you voluntarily provide it.
