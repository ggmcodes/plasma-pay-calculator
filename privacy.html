Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we protect your data and respect your peaceful productivity journey.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to improve our website and content. This includes basic analytics such as page views, session duration, and general geographic information (country/region level only).

We do not collect personal identifying information like names, addresses, or detailed location data unless you voluntarily provide it (such as subscribing to our newsletter).

Cookies and Tracking

Essential Cookies: Our website uses essential cookies for basic functionality, such as remembering your timer preferences and ensuring the site works properly.

Analytics Cookies: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This helps us improve the user experience and create better content for your study sessions.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect some site functionality.

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to track website usage. Google's privacy policy governs how they handle this data.

Google AdSense: We may display ads through Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to show relevant ads based on your browsing history.

YouTube: Our video content is hosted on YouTube. When you watch our videos, YouTube's privacy policy applies.

Affiliate Links and Monetization

Some product recommendations include affiliate links. This means we may earn a small commission if you make a purchase through our links, at no extra cost to you.

We only recommend products we genuinely use and believe will enhance your study experience. Affiliate earnings help us maintain the website and create more content.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect any information collected. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We store minimal data and rely on trusted third-party services (Google) for data handling, which have their own robust security measures.

Your Rights

Opt-out: You can disable cookies in your browser settings and opt out of Google Analytics tracking using Google's opt-out tools.

Data Access: Since we collect minimal data, most of what we have is aggregated analytics. If you have questions about your data, contact us through our YouTube channel.

Newsletter: You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email.

Questions About Privacy

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle data, please reach out to us:

YouTube: @SunsetStudyClub - Leave a comment or send a message

We typically respond to privacy inquiries within 48 hours.
