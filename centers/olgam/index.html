Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about donating at Olgam Life

How long does a plasma donation take at Olgam Life?
Your first visit takes about 2 hours including the health screening and paperwork. Return visits typically take 45 minutes to 1 hour depending on your plasma volume.
How often can I donate plasma?
You can donate plasma up to twice per week with at least one day between donations. This means you can donate up to 8 times per month for maximum earnings.
How do I get paid at Olgam Life?
Payment is loaded onto a prepaid debit card immediately after each donation. You can use it anywhere Visa/Mastercard is accepted or withdraw cash at ATMs.
Do Olgam Life centers have Spanish-speaking staff?
Yes! Most Olgam Life locations have bilingual staff who speak both English and Spanish to serve the diverse NYC community.
What should I bring to my first appointment?
Bring a valid government-issued photo ID, proof of address (utility bill or bank statement), and your Social Security card or tax ID. Make sure you're well-hydrated and have eaten a healthy meal.
