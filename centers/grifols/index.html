Grifols Biomat USA Donation Requirements

✅ Basic Requirements

  • Age: 18-65 years old
  • Weight: Minimum 110 lbs
  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Social Security number
  • Proof of current address

🏥 Health Requirements

  • Complete medical evaluation
  • Normal blood pressure
  • Adequate protein levels
  • No recent tattoos or piercings
  • No high-risk travel history

⏰ Donation Process

  • Up to 2 donations per week
  • 48-hour minimum between donations
  • 45-75 minutes per session
  • 8 donations maximum per month
  • Walk-ins and appointments welcome

Grifols Plasma FAQ

How much does Grifols pay?

$40-$85 per regular visit; first donations pay $60-$100 plus $75-$125 in bonus pay. See the full Grifols pay chart.

What is the new donor bonus?

Up to $800 across your first 4 donations, plus a $50 refer-a-friend bonus and seasonal promotions.

Is Grifols the same as Biomat USA?

Yes, Biomat USA centers are owned and operated by Grifols. Read the full explanation.

How often can you donate?

Up to twice per week with at least 48 hours between visits, up to 8 donations per month.
