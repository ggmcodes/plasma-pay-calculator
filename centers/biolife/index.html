BioLife Plasma Donation Requirements

✅ Basic Requirements

  • Age: 18-64 years old
  • Weight: Minimum 110 lbs
  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Social Security number
  • Proof of current address

🏥 Health Requirements

  • Complete medical screening
  • Normal vital signs
  • Adequate iron levels
  • No disqualifying medications
  • No recent travel restrictions
