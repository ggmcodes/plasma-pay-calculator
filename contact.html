Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How accurate are your plasma center rates?
A: We update our rates weekly based on multiple sources including center websites, donor reports, and direct verification. However, rates change frequently, so we always recommend calling the center to confirm current rates before visiting.
Q: Do you store my personal information from the calculator?
A: No, all calculations are performed locally in your browser. We do not collect or store any personal information from our calculator or eligibility quiz.
Q: Can you help me with medical questions about plasma donation?
A: We cannot provide medical advice. All health and eligibility questions should be directed to qualified medical professionals or the plasma center's medical staff.
Q: How can I report incorrect center information?
A: Please email us at rates@plasmapaycalculator.com with the center name, location, and the correct information. Include any supporting documentation if available.
Q: Do you have plasma centers in my area?
A: Check our Centers Directory which covers all 50 states. If you don't see centers in your area, email us your location and we'll research options for you.
Q: Can you recommend the best plasma center for me?
A: We provide information and comparisons, but the best center depends on your individual needs, location, and circumstances. Use our comparison tools and consider factors like location, rates, and convenience.
