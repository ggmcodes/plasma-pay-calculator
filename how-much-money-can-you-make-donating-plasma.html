Frequently Asked Questions

How much money can you make donating plasma?

You can make $50-100 per plasma donation depending on your weight and location. Most donors earn $500-600 monthly by donating twice per week. New donors can earn $800-1,200 their first month with bonuses.

How much do you get paid for plasma donation?

Plasma donation pays $50-100 per session. Payment depends on your weight category: Under 150 lbs earns $50, 150-174 lbs earns $60, and 175+ lbs earns $70 per donation.

How often can you donate plasma for money?

You can donate plasma up to 2 times per week with at least 48 hours between donations. This allows for maximum earnings of $200-400 per week.

Which plasma centers pay the most?

CSL Plasma typically offers the highest new donor bonuses ($1,200 first month), while BioLife and Octapharma offer competitive regular rates and premium location bonuses.
