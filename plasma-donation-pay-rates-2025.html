Pay Rate FAQs

What are the current plasma donation pay rates for 2025?

2025 plasma donation pay rates range from $50-100 per donation. CSL Plasma pays $50-70, BioLife pays $60-80, and Octapharma pays $55-75 depending on weight and location.

Which plasma center has the highest pay rates?

BioLife typically has the highest regular pay rates at $60-80 per donation. However, CSL Plasma offers the highest new donor bonuses up to $1,200 first month.

Do plasma pay rates vary by location?

Yes, pay rates vary significantly by location. College towns pay 15-30% more than rural areas due to higher demand and competition between centers.

How often do plasma pay rates change?

Pay rates typically change monthly or seasonally. Centers often increase rates during summer months and college seasons when demand is higher.
