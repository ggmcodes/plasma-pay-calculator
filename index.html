Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I earn donating plasma in 2025?

New donors can earn $50-120 per visit with first-time bonuses reaching $900-1,200 in the first month. Regular donors typically earn $500-700 monthly donating twice weekly. Rates vary by center, location, and weight category.

Which plasma centers pay the highest rates?

CSL Plasma, BioLife, and Octapharma Plasma typically offer the highest rates nationwide. CSL Plasma averages $60-100 per visit, BioLife offers $50-90, and new donor bonuses can reach $900+ in major cities.

How often can I donate plasma for maximum earnings?

FDA regulations allow plasma donation twice per week with at least 48 hours between donations. This means you can donate up to 8-9 times per month, maximizing your monthly earnings to $500-700 for regular donors.

Are plasma donation earnings taxable income?

Yes, plasma donation income is taxable and must be reported to the IRS. Centers issue 1099-MISC forms for earnings over $600 annually. Track your donations and related expenses for tax purposes.
