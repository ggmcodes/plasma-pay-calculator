NYC Donor Tips & Transportation

üöá Getting There

  • All centers within 3 blocks of subway stations
  • Use MetroCard or OMNY for easy transit
  • Plan for 2-3 hours total visit time
  • Avoid rush hours (7-9 AM, 5-7 PM) if possible

üí° NYC Donor Tips

  • Higher COL = higher pay rates citywide
  • Manhattan centers typically pay most
  • Book appointments online to avoid waits
  • Bring entertainment for longer waits

üìã Required Documents

  • Valid government-issued photo ID
  • Social Security card or W-2
  • Proof of NYC address (utility bill)
  • All documents must be originals
