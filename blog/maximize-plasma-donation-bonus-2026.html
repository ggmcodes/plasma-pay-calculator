Bonus Maximization Strategies

1. Choose the Best New Donor Promotion

Before your first donation, compare current new donor offers at every center within reasonable distance. A 20-minute drive could mean $100+ more for your first month.

Action: Call 3-4 centers and ask "What's the total I can earn from my first 8 donations with current promotions?"

2. Never Miss an App Bonus

Download your center's app immediately. Enable ALL notifications. Flash bonuses often have short windows (24-48 hours).

Action: Check app every morning. Many flash bonuses are announced between 8-10 AM.

3. Maximize Referrals

Every person you know who could donate is $50-100 in your pocket. Friends, family, coworkers, classmates‚Äîshare your referral code widely.

Action: Post about your experience on social media. Mention the new donor bonus and your referral code.

4. Hit Frequency Milestones

Many centers offer bonuses for donating twice in one week or 8 times in a month. Structure your schedule to hit these milestones.

Action: Plan donations for Monday and Thursday (or similar 3-4 day spacing) to maximize weekly bonuses.

5. Time Your Donations Strategically

Centers often run promotions at predictable times: beginning of month, holidays, and when supply is low (January, summer).

Action: Start donating in January or June/July when new donor bonuses tend to peak.
