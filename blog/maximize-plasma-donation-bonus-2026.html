Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch centers to get another new donor bonus? ‚ñº

No. Plasma centers share a national database (NDDR). Once you've completed the new donor program at one center, you won't qualify at another. Choose wisely for your first center‚Äîit's the only time you'll get the new donor bonus.

What happens if I miss the new donor deadline? ‚ñº

Unfortunately, most centers are strict about deadlines. If you complete 7 donations by day 30 but miss the 8th, you typically forfeit the remaining bonus. Plan ahead and prioritize hitting all 8 donations within the window.

Do referral bonuses expire? ‚ñº

Referral codes typically don't expire, but your referred person usually needs to donate within 30-60 days of their first visit for you to receive the bonus. Follow up with your referrals to make sure they complete the required donations.

How do I find out about flash bonuses? ‚ñº

Download your center's official app and enable all notifications. Also check your email daily (including spam folder) and follow your center on social media. Some donors join local Facebook groups or Reddit communities where others share current promotions.

Can I stack multiple bonuses on one visit? ‚ñº

Yes! Most bonuses stack. You can earn your base pay + app check-in bonus + flash promotion + frequency bonus all in one visit. This is why optimized donors can earn $70-100+ on a single donation while unoptimized donors earn $40-50.

Should I donate more if I'm at a higher weight tier? ‚ñº

Higher weight donors automatically give more plasma and get paid more per donation. If you're close to a weight threshold (e.g., 148 lbs vs 150 lbs), the difference can be $5-10 per donation. However, don't try to game this‚Äîdonate at your natural weight.
