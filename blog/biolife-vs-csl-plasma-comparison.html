BioLife vs CSL Plasma 2025: Which Pays More? Complete Comparison

Head-to-head comparison of BioLife and CSL Plasma pay rates, bonuses, locations, and donor experience. Find out which plasma center chain pays more in 2025.

🏢 Chain Overview

BioLife and CSL Plasma are the two largest plasma donation center chains in the United States, operating over 500 centers combined. Both offer competitive compensation, but they have different approaches to donor rewards and experiences.

  • Parent Company:Takeda Pharmaceutical
  • Founded:2009
  • Locations:220+ centers across 35+ states
  • Focus:Technology-driven donor experience
  • Specialty:Student-friendly locations
  • Parent Company:CSL Behring (Australia)
  • Founded:2000
  • Locations:300+ centers in 40+ states
  • Focus:High-volume operations
  • Specialty:Aggressive new donor bonuses

💰 Pay Rate Comparison

CSL Plasma consistently offers $5-10 more per donation across all weight categories, with particularly strong rates for donors over 175 lbs.

  • First donation/week:Lower base rate
  • Second donation/week:Higher completion rate
  • Weight-based tiers:Higher weight = more pay
  • Loyalty bonuses:Monthly completion rewards
  • Weight-based pricing:Aggressive rates for heavier donors
  • Frequency bonuses:Rewards for twice-weekly donations
  • Promotional periods:Regular bonus campaigns
  • Return donor incentives:Come-back bonuses
