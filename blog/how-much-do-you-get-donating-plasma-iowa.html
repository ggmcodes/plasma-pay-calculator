Iowa Plasma Donation Requirements

📋 Basic Requirements

  • ✅ Age 18-65 years old
  • ✅ Weight 110+ pounds
  • ✅ Valid photo ID
  • ✅ Social Security card
  • ✅ Proof of Iowa address

🏥 Health Standards

  • ✅ Blood pressure 90/50-180/100
  • ✅ Pulse 50-100 BPM
  • ✅ Temperature under 99.5°F
  • ✅ Protein level 6.0+ g/dL
  • ✅ Hematocrit 38%+ (women), 40%+ (men)

⏰ Time Commitment

  • • First visit: 2-3 hours
  • • Return visits: 1-2 hours
  • • Donation time: 30-45 minutes
  • • Recovery time: 15 minutes
  • • Max 2 visits per week

📍 Iowa-Specific Note

Iowa residents can donate at any center nationwide, but you'll need proof of Iowa residence for new donor bonuses. Most centers accept utility bills, bank statements, or lease agreements dated within 30 days.
