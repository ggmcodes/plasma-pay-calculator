Florida Plasma Donation Tips

Beat the Florida Heat

  • Hydrate extra - Florida humidity increases dehydration
  • Donate in the morning - Cooler temps, less crowded
  • Avoid afternoon appointments - Peak heat hours
  • Bring cold water - Stay hydrated waiting

Maximize Florida Earnings

  1. Start with Octapharma - $1,500 first month bonus
  2. Switch to BioLife - Best ongoing rates ($880/month max)
  3. Donate during tourist season - Centers often run extra promos
  4. Use referral bonuses - Lots of snowbirds to refer!
  5. Check hurricane season promos - Centers sometimes offer bonuses
