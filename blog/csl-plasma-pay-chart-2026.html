Why Weight Matters

The FDA allows plasma centers to collect a volume proportional to your body weight. Heavier donors can safely give more plasma, which is why they're compensated at higher rates. A 200 lb donor may give up to 880 mL per donation, while a 110 lb donor gives approximately 690 mL.
