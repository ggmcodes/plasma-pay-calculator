About BPL Plasma

BPL Plasma (Bio Products Laboratory) is a UK-based pharmaceutical company that operates plasma collection centers in the United States. With about 40 locations, primarily in the Southeast, BPL Plasma is smaller than major competitors but offers competitive rates in its markets.
