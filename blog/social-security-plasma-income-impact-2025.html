‚ùì Emergency Benefit Questions

What if I already earned too much from plasma?

URGENT: Report immediately to minimize overpayment. SSA offers payment plans and potential waivers for financial hardship.

Can I donate plasma if I'm on disability?

Yes, but you must stay under income limits and report ALL earnings. Even $1 over the limit can trigger benefit loss.

Does plasma income count as "work" for disability reviews?

YES! Regular plasma donation can trigger medical reviews and suggestions that you're capable of more work than claimed.

What if my spouse donates plasma?

Spouse income generally doesn't count against your individual benefits, but can affect household calculations for some programs.
