New Orleans Plasma Overview

New Orleans has 8+ plasma donation centers in the metro area. The unique city offers affordable living and a service industry workforce that benefits from extra income.

New Orleans Donor Tips

  1. Mardi Gras season: Centers crowded before/after - people need cash
  2. Summer hydration: NOLA heat and humidity require extra water
  3. Saints game days: Avoid downtown/Superdome area
  4. Service industry: Donate between restaurant shifts
  5. Festival prep: Donate ahead of Jazz Fest, French Quarter Fest

NOLA Service Economy + Plasma

New Orleans' hospitality economy makes plasma valuable:

  • Restaurant workers: Supplement tip income
  • Musicians: Consistent income between gigs
  • Tourism workers: Bridge slow seasons
