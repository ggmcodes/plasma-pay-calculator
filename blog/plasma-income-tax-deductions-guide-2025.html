❓ Frequently Asked Tax Questions

Can I deduct mileage if I combine trips?

YES! You can deduct mileage for the business portion of combined trips. If you donate plasma then go grocery shopping, the plasma portion is deductible.

What if I get audited for claiming these deductions?

With proper documentation and legitimate business activity, these deductions are defensible. Keep detailed records and maintain profit motive.

Can I claim home office deduction for record keeping?

Yes, if you use part of your home exclusively for plasma business activities (record keeping, scheduling, research), you can claim home office deduction.

How far back can I amend returns to claim missed deductions?

Generally 3 years from the original filing date. File Form 1040X for amended returns to claim missed deductions.
