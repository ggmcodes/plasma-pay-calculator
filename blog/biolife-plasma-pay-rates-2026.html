BioLife Promotions 2026

BioLife runs frequent promotions throughout the year:

  • Buddy Bonus: Earn $50-$100 for each friend you refer
  • Comeback Bonus: $200-$400 for returning after 6+ months
  • Holiday Bonuses: Extra pay during holidays (Thanksgiving, Christmas)
  • Summer promotions: Often higher rates June-August
  • App coupons: Check the BioLife app for surprise bonuses

BioLife App Rewards

The BioLife Plasma app offers additional benefits:

  • Digital payments: Funds loaded to BioLife debit card
  • Appointment scheduling: Book online, reduce wait times
  • Coupon codes: Exclusive promotions in the app
  • Donation tracking: See your history and earnings

  1. Donate twice per week - Maximum allowed is 2x/week
  2. Check the app daily - Surprise bonuses appear frequently

  5. Watch for comeback offers - If you pause, wait for a good comeback bonus
