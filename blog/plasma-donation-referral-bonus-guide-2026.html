Quick Answer

Plasma referral bonuses pay $25-100 per successful referral. Share your unique code from the center's app. Your friend must use the code during registration and complete required donations (usually 1-3). Both referrer and referee typically receive bonuses.

How Plasma Referral Programs Work

Basic Process

  1. Get your unique referral code from the center's app
  2. Share the code with friends interested in donating
  3. Friend uses code during registration
  4. Friend completes required donations
  5. Both receive bonus payments

Best Practices

Successful Referral Strategy

  1. Share your honest experience
  2. Explain the process and pay clearly
  3. Help friends prepare for first visit
  4. Ensure they use your code at registration
  5. Follow up to encourage completion

Who to Refer

  • Friends who meet basic eligibility (18+, 110+ lbs)
  • People interested in extra income
  • Students, gig workers, others with flexible time
  • People you know will actually follow through

Referral Tips

Sharing Your Code

  • Keep your code easily accessible
  • Share via text for easy copy/paste
  • Remind friends to enter at registration
  • Double-check they used it correctly

Helping Friends Succeed

  • Share preparation tips (hydration, food, documents)
  • Tell them what to expect first visit
  • Encourage them to complete qualifying donations
  • Answer questions from your experience

Watch for Promotions

  • Centers sometimes increase referral bonuses
  • Holiday promotions may offer more
  • Check app regularly for special offers
  • Time referrals during enhanced promotions

Common Problems & Solutions

Friend Forgot to Use Code

  • Contact center immediately
  • May be able to add if caught quickly
  • Usually can't fix after processing
  • Prevention is best - remind them beforehand

Bonus Not Received

  • Verify friend completed required donations
  • Check if time limit was met
  • Confirm code was properly entered
  • Contact center customer service

Referral Denied

  • Friend may have donated there before
  • Requirements may not have been met
  • Code may have been entered incorrectly
  • Ask center for specific reason

Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I earn from plasma referral bonuses?

Referral bonuses typically range from $25-100 per successful referral. Some centers offer higher amounts during promotions. Both you and your friend may receive bonuses.

How do plasma referral programs work?

Share your referral code with friends. When they register and complete required donations, you both receive bonuses. Requirements vary by center but typically require 1-3 completed donations.

When do I get paid for plasma referrals?

Payment timing varies. Some centers pay after the referred person's first donation, others after 2-3 donations. Payment is usually loaded to your plasma card within days of qualifying.

Where do I find my plasma referral code?

Find your referral code in the center's app under 'Refer a Friend' or similar section. Some centers provide physical referral cards. You can also ask staff for your code.

Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?

Most centers have no limit on referrals, though some may cap bonuses during specific promotions. You can potentially earn significant extra income through regular referrals.

What if my friend forgets to use my referral code?

Contact the center quickly. Some may be able to add the code retroactively if your friend registered recently. Once processed without code, it's usually too late.
