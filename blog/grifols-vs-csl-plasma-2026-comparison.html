Rewards Programs: iGive vs Kedrewards

CSL Plasma iGive Rewards

  • Points per donation: Earn points on every visit
  • Bonus points: For completing monthly donation goals
  • Redemption: Points can be redeemed for extra payment
  • Tiers: Higher tiers = better bonus rates
  • App integration: Track points in CSL app

Grifols Kedrewards

  • Points per donation: Earn on every visit
  • Bonus opportunities: Monthly challenges
  • Redemption: Convert to additional payment

Rewards Winner

Our Verdict: Which Should You Choose?

Choose Grifols If:

  • You prefer walk-in flexibility
  • CSL isn't conveniently located
  • You want simpler scheduling
  • Your local Grifols has good reviews

Choose CSL Plasma If:

  • You'll use the iGive rewards program
  • You prefer booking appointments

Overall Winner: CSL Plasma

For most donors, CSL Plasma offers better value:

  • $40-120 more per month in base pay
  • Better rewards program (iGive)

However, if walk-in flexibility matters to you, Grifols is the better choice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which company is larger?

Both have 300+ US locations. CSL is the larger company globally (Australian-based), while Grifols is Spanish-based and equally established.

Can I switch from Grifols to CSL?

Yes, but you can only be active at one center at a time. Complete any bonus programs before switching.

Do they share a database?

All plasma centers share a national database (NDDR) to prevent double-donating, regardless of company.
