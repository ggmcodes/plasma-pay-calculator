Des Moines Plasma Donation FAQ

Which Des Moines plasma center pays the most?

CSL Plasma offers the highest pay in Des Moines at $50-$100 per visit, plus a $1,000 new donor bonus. Both Des Moines CSL locations (East University and SE 14th) offer identical rates.

Can Drake University students donate plasma?

Yes! Many Drake students donate at CSL East (closest to campus) or BioLife West Des Moines. Both centers offer student-friendly hours and Drake students frequently use plasma income for textbooks and expenses.

What documents do I need in Des Moines?

Valid photo ID, Social Security card, and proof of Iowa address (utility bill, bank statement, or lease agreement within 30 days). All Des Moines centers accept the same documentation.

How long does the first visit take in Des Moines?

First visits typically take 2-3 hours including screening, physical exam, and first donation. CSL locations tend to be busiest, while Grifols often has the fastest processing times.
