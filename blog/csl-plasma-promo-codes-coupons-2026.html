CSL Plasma Promo Code FAQ

Are these promo codes legitimate?

These codes are user-reported and compiled from various sources. They change frequently. Always verify with your local CSL Plasma center.

Why isn't my code working?

Possible reasons: Code expired, region-specific, already used, typo, or not applicable to your donor status. Ask center staff for help.

Can I use multiple codes?

Generally only one promo code at signup. Referral bonuses can often stack with new donor bonuses. iGive point codes may work separately.

How do I get the $1000 bonus?

Complete 8 donations in your first month as a new donor. The bonus is paid incrementally with each donation.

What's the difference between promo codes and iGive codes?

Promo codes typically give cash bonuses. iGive codes add points to your rewards account, which can be redeemed for gift cards or cash.
