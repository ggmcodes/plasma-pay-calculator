Unlike Texas or Florida, New York has state income tax (4-10.9%). Factor this into your earnings expectations. Plasma income is taxable.

New York Plasma Donation Tips

NYC-Specific Tips

  • Use public transit: Parking is expensive; most centers are subway-accessible
  • Go early or late: Avoid peak commute hours for shorter waits
  • Check multiple boroughs: Brooklyn centers often have better bonuses
  • Winter donations: Bundle up, but the centers are warm!

Maximize New York Earnings

  1. Start with Octapharma - Highest first-month bonus ($1,500)
  2. Switch to BioLife - Best ongoing rates after new donor period
  3. Use referral codes - Earn $50-$100 per friend
  4. Track promotions - NY centers compete aggressively
  5. Consider Upstate - If you're near the border, check Upstate centers
