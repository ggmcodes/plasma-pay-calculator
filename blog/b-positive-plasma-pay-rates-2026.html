Tips to Maximize Your B Positive Earnings

1

Donate Twice Weekly Consistently

B Positive pays significantly more for second weekly donations ($60-$80 vs $40-$60). Maintain a consistent Tuesday/Thursday or Monday/Friday schedule to maximize the higher second-donation rate each week.

2

Download and Monitor the App

B Positive frequently runs surprise bonus weeks advertised only through their mobile app. Donors who check the app regularly can earn an extra $100-$200 monthly by catching these limited-time promotions.

3

Optimize Your Donation Time

Hydrate heavily the day before and morning of donation. Drink 64+ ounces of water in the 24 hours before donating. Well-hydrated donors complete donations 10-15 minutes faster, allowing for more convenient scheduling.

4

Leverage the Referral Program

With $25-$75 per successful referral, bringing 4-5 friends annually can add $100-$375 to your yearly earnings. Share your experience on social media or with coworkers to maximize referral opportunities.

5

Maintain Iron and Protein Levels

Deferrals due to low hemoglobin cost you money. Eat iron-rich foods (red meat, spinach, fortified cereals) and consider an iron supplement. Consume 75-100g of protein daily to maintain eligibility.

6

Use Mobile Check-In

Check in via the B Positive app 30 minutes before arrival to reduce wait time. This feature puts you in the digital queue, often cutting 10-20 minutes off your total visit time.
