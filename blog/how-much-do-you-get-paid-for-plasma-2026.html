Frequently Asked Questions

Do you get paid the same day?

Yes! Most centers load payment onto a prepaid debit card immediately after your donation.

Is plasma money taxable?

Technically yes, plasma compensation is considered income. However, centers don't issue 1099s for amounts under $600, and enforcement is minimal.

How often can you donate?

Up to twice per week, with at least one day between donations. That's a maximum of 8 times per month.

How long does it take?

First visit: 2-3 hours (includes screening). Return visits: 45 minutes to 1.5 hours.
