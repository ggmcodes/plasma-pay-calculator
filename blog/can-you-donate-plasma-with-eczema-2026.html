Can You Donate Plasma With Eczema?

Yes, eczema usually doesn't disqualify you from plasma donation in 2026, but location matters:

  • Arm eczema: May need to use other arm or wait
  • Other body areas: Usually not a problem
  • Active infection: Wait until healed
  • Most treatments OK: Topical steroids acceptable

Why Eczema Usually Doesn't Disqualify

  • Not contagious: Can't spread through plasma
  • Skin condition only: Doesn't affect blood
  • Common condition: Millions have it and donate
  • Location-dependent: Many body areas don't matter

Tips for Eczema Donors

  1. Manage flares: Keep eczema controlled before donating
  2. Moisturize: Well-hydrated skin handles bandages better
  3. Avoid triggers: Don't flare up your donation arm
  4. Tell the staff: They can use hypoallergenic tape
  5. Alternate arms: If one arm is clear, use that one
  6. Timing: Donate when your skin is at its best

Bandage Considerations

After donation, you'll need a bandage:

  • Standard tape: May irritate sensitive skin
  • Ask for alternatives: Paper tape, hypoallergenic options
  • Self-adhering wrap: Coban doesn't use adhesive
  • Remove gently: Don't tear skin

Frequently Asked Questions

I have eczema on both inner elbows - can I still donate?

It depends on severity. If the skin is intact (not open, weeping, or infected), you may still be able to donate. The staff will assess the venipuncture site. If both arms are significantly affected, you may need to wait until the eczema clears.

Will the alcohol wipe make my eczema worse?

Possibly. Alcohol can be drying and irritating to eczema-prone skin. Let the staff know about your eczema - they need to clean the area but can be gentle.

I use Dupixent - can I donate?

This varies by center. Dupixent is a biologic medication. Some centers accept it, others may defer. Call ahead to check your specific center's policy.

Good News

Eczema in most body locations doesn't prevent plasma donation. Keep your donation arm clear and your eczema well-managed, and you should be able to donate.
