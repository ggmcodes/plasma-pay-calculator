How Plasma Referrals Work

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Get your code: Find your unique referral code in your center's app
  2. Share: Send code to friends who've never donated at that center
  3. Friend signs up: They use your code when registering
  4. Friend donates: They complete required number of donations
  5. You get paid: Bonus added to your account (usually within 24-48 hours)

Important Rules

Requirements
  • Friend must be new to that specific center chain
  • You must be an active donor (donated within 6 months)
  • Friend must use your code at signup - can't add later
  • Friend must complete required donations successfully

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I refer someone to a different center than mine?

No. Referral codes only work at the same center chain. Your BioLife code won't work at CSL.

What if my friend donates at a different location?

That's fine! As long as it's the same center chain (e.g., any BioLife location), you'll get credit.

How do I know if my referral was credited?

Check your app or online account. Most centers show pending and completed referral bonuses in the "Rewards" or "Referral" section.

Can I refer family members?

Usually yes. There's typically no restriction on referring family, just friends who've never donated at that center.

What if my friend already donated somewhere else?

They can still be "new" to a different center. Someone who donated at CSL could be referred to BioLife as a new donor.

Do referral bonuses expire?

The bonus is credited once your friend completes the required donations. There's usually no time limit, but your friend should use the code when signing up - it can't be added later.
