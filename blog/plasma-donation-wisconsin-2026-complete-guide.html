Wisconsin Plasma Donation Tips

Winter Donation Tips

  • Stay hydrated indoors: Wisconsin winters are dry and heated indoor air dehydrates
  • Warm up before: Cold constricts veins - arrive early, warm hands
  • Wear layers: Easy to adjust in sometimes-cold centers
  • Watch weather: Blizzards may close centers - call ahead

Maximize Wisconsin Earnings

  1. Compare Milwaukee options - 12+ centers means competition
  2. Check Chicago border - Northern IL centers accessible
  3. Use referral codes - College towns = lots of potential
  4. Game days: Avoid Packers game days at Green Bay centers
