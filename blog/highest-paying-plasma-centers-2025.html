üö® Red Flags: Centers to Avoid

Warning Signs of Low-Paying Centers

  • No published rates - Legitimate centers are transparent about pay
  • Bait and switch tactics - Different rates than advertised
  • Excessive wait times - Poor management affects your hourly earnings
  • Hidden fees - Charges for cards, processing, etc.
  • Poor facility conditions - May indicate financial problems

How to Verify Center Legitimacy

  1. Check FDA registration - All centers must be FDA approved
  2. Read recent reviews - Focus on payment and wait time complaints
  3. Call and ask specific questions - Legitimate centers answer honestly
  4. Visit in person - Check facility cleanliness and organization
