Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a BioLife donation take?

First-time visits take 2-3 hours including health screening. Return visits typically take 45-90 minutes depending on your flow rate and center capacity.

Does BioLife pay cash or debit card?

BioLife loads payment onto a prepaid Visa debit card immediately after your donation. You can use it anywhere Visa is accepted or withdraw cash at ATMs.

Can I donate at BioLife if I have tattoos?

Yes, but tattoos must be fully healed (at least 3-4 months old) and obtained from a licensed facility. Some states have different waiting periods.

Why does BioLife pay different amounts each visit?

BioLife uses a tiered payment system where your second weekly donation pays more than your first. This incentivizes regular, consistent donations which are important for plasma supply.
