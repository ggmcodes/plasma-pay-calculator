Frequently Asked Questions About BioLife Plasma Pay

How much does BioLife pay for first-time donors?

BioLife pays new donors $900-$1,200 for their first month (8 donations). The exact amount varies by location, with major cities offering higher rates. Your first donation typically pays $100-$125.

Does BioLife report to the IRS?

Yes, BioLife reports payments over $600 per year to the IRS. You'll receive a 1099-MISC form if you exceed this threshold. Plasma donation income is considered taxable compensation.

How often can I donate at BioLife?

You can donate plasma twice per week at BioLife, with at least 48 hours between donations. The maximum is 104 donations per year. Most donors follow a Monday/Thursday or Tuesday/Friday schedule.

Does BioLife pay more for AB plasma?

Some BioLife locations offer AB Elite programs paying $10-20 extra per donation for AB blood type donors. AB plasma is universal and in high demand. Check with your local center for AB Elite availability.

What's the BioLife debit card?

BioLife loads your payment onto a prepaid Mastercard debit card immediately after each donation. The card has no monthly fees and can be used anywhere Mastercard is accepted or at ATMs.

Can I switch from CSL to BioLife?

Yes! If you haven't donated at BioLife in 6+ months, you may qualify for returning donor bonuses ($600-$800). There's no waiting period to switch between plasma centers.
