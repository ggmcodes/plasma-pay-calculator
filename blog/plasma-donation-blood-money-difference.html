🤔 WHY Does Plasma Pay But Blood Doesn't?

🧪 Plasma is a BUSINESS

  • 💰 Pharmaceutical companies buy plasma to make life-saving medications
  • 🏭 Manufacturing process turns plasma into drugs worth thousands
  • 📈 High demand for plasma proteins creates competitive market
  • 🔄 Frequent donations needed due to plasma regeneration

❤️ Blood is VOLUNTEER Service

  • 🏥 Non-profit model - Red Cross and hospitals don't pay donors
  • 🎯 Direct transfusion - blood goes straight to patients
  • ⚖️ FDA regulations prohibit payment for blood donations
  • 🕒 Less frequent donations due to red cell recovery time
