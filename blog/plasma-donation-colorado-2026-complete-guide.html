Colorado Plasma Donation Tips

Altitude Advantages

  • Higher hemoglobin: Living at altitude naturally increases iron levels
  • Easier qualification: Less likely to be deferred for low hemoglobin
  • Stay hydrated: Altitude increases dehydration - drink extra water

Weather Considerations

  • Winter storms: Centers may have reduced hours during blizzards
  • Dry climate: Stay extra hydrated year-round
  • Sudden weather: Check forecasts before appointments

Maximize Colorado Earnings

  1. Start with Octapharma - $1,500 first-month bonus
  2. Compare Aurora options - High center density = competitive rates
  3. Use referral codes - College towns have lots of potential referrals
  4. Donate consistently - Colorado's dry air makes hydration crucial
