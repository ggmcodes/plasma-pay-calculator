Pro Tips for Maximizing Grifols Bonuses

Donation Timing Strategies

  • Donate on promotional days: Check monthly calendar and schedule around high-bonus dates
  • Weekend donations: If weekend bonuses are offered, use Saturday slots to stack bonuses

Avoiding Common Bonus Mistakes

  • Missing deadline by one day: Track your 45-day new donor window carefully
  • Not asking about promotions: Staff may not automatically apply all bonuses - ask what's available
  • Skipping the app: Many exclusive promotions only appear in iGive Rewards
  • Ignoring communication: Opt-in to texts/emails for bonus alerts
  • Forgetting referral codes: Tell friends to mention your name/code at registration
  • Deferrals due to preventable issues: Hydrate well, eat protein, get adequate sleep

Hydration and Preparation for Faster Donations

Faster donations mean more time back in your day and ability to donate on tight schedules:

  • Drink 64+ oz water in the 24 hours before donation
  • Eat protein-rich meal 2-3 hours before (lean meat, eggs, beans)
  • Avoid fatty foods 24 hours before (can cause cloudy plasma/deferral)
  • Exercise hand grips during donation to improve flow
  • Stay warm - cold constricts veins and slows donation

Optimal preparation can reduce donation time from 90 minutes to 45-60 minutes, making it easier to fit donations into your schedule and hit frequency bonuses.
