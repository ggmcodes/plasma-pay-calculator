Frequently Asked Questions

How much does KEDPLASMA pay for plasma in 2026?
KEDPLASMA pays $35-$70 per donation depending on your weight and location. New donors can earn $600-$800 in their first month through special bonus programs. Regular donors typically earn $50-$60 per visit.
What is KEDPLASMA's new donor bonus?
KEDPLASMA new donor bonuses for 2026 range from $600-$800 for your first 8 donations within 30-45 days. Some locations offer up to $100 per visit for the first few donations. Check your local center for current promotions.
Is KEDPLASMA better than CSL or BioLife?
KEDPLASMA offers competitive pay similar to CSL and BioLife. While KEDPLASMA has fewer locations (35+ vs 300+), they often have shorter wait times and can offer comparable or better rates in areas where they compete directly with larger chains.
Where is KEDPLASMA located?
KEDPLASMA has 35+ locations across the United States, primarily in the Midwest, South, and East Coast. States with KEDPLASMA include Texas, Florida, Ohio, Georgia, North Carolina, Indiana, and several others. Visit kedplasma.com to find your nearest location.
How often can I donate at KEDPLASMA?
You can donate plasma at KEDPLASMA up to twice per week (every 7 days), with at least one day between donations. This follows FDA guidelines. Most donors donate 6-8 times per month to maximize earnings.
How long does a KEDPLASMA donation take?
First-time donations take 2-3 hours including paperwork, health screening, and the donation itself. Return visits typically take 45-90 minutes. KEDPLASMA often has shorter wait times than larger chains due to smaller facility sizes.
