Georgia Plasma Donation Tips

Beat the Georgia Heat

  • Stay extra hydrated - Georgia humidity can be deceptive
  • Donate early morning - Before the heat of the day
  • Avoid afternoon appointments in summer
  • Bring a cold water bottle for after donation

Maximize Georgia Earnings

  1. Start with Octapharma - Highest first-month bonus ($1,500)
  2. Switch to BioLife - Best ongoing rates after new donor period
  3. Check location-specific promos - Atlanta centers compete heavily
  4. Use referral codes - Georgia has lots of newcomers
  5. Consider college town centers - Often have student promos
