Frequently Asked Questions

Does Grifols or CSL Plasma pay more in 2026?

For regular donors, Grifols (Biomat USA) pays more with rates up to $100 per donation compared to CSL's $50-100 range. However, CSL Plasma offers significantly higher new donor bonuses (up to $1,000 vs $600). Long-term donors earn more at Grifols; new donors earn more at CSL.

What is Grifols Biomat USA?

Grifols Biomat USA is the plasma collection division of Grifols, a Spanish pharmaceutical company. It operates plasma donation centers primarily in the United States under the Biomat USA brand. Grifols is known for offering some of the highest per-donation pay rates in the industry.

How does the Grifols Passport program compare to CSL iGive?

Grifols Passport offers weekly "Early Bird" bonuses ($5) and monthly donation threshold bonuses ($25-35), but bonuses must be manually claimed with staff. CSL iGive focuses on referral bonuses ($100 per referral) and is automatically applied. CSL is better for referrals; Grifols is better for consistent donors.

Which has more locations - Grifols or CSL Plasma?

CSL Plasma has significantly more locations with 300+ centers in 45 states compared to Grifols' 180+ locations in 32 states. CSL offers broader nationwide coverage, while Grifols focuses on specific markets, particularly in Texas, California, and the Southwest.

What is the maximum you can earn at Grifols vs CSL Plasma?

At Grifols, committed donors can earn up to $800+ monthly ($9,600 annually) with the highest per-donation rates. CSL Plasma donors can earn $400-800 monthly ($5,200-9,600 annually). Grifols has higher earning potential for dedicated long-term donors who weigh 175+ lbs.
