Oregon Plasma Donation Tips

Maximize Oregon Earnings

  1. Check Vancouver WA - Same metro, sometimes different promos
  2. Use referral programs - College towns = easy referrals
  3. Winter donating - Rainy season means less competition
  4. Book ahead - Portland centers get busy

Oregon-Specific Notes

  • No sales tax: Your payment card works the same everywhere
  • Weather: Centers open rain or shine (this is Oregon!)
  • Transit: TriMet serves many Portland-area centers
