Understanding Grifols Plasma Brands

Grifols is one of the world's largest plasma collection companies, operating over 300 centers across the United States under multiple brand names:

Biomat USA

The primary Grifols brand with 200+ locations nationwide. Most common in southern and western states.

Talecris Plasma

Former independent company acquired by Grifols. 80+ locations, primarily in eastern states.

Interstate Blood Bank

Grifols subsidiary operating specialized collection centers in select markets.

Important: All Grifols brands follow similar pay structures, but rates vary by location. Always check your local center for current promotions.

Returning Donor Bonuses & Loyalty Programs

Lapsed Donor Bonus

Haven't donated in 6+ months? Grifols often treats returning donors as "new" donors, qualifying you for the full new donor bonus again. Check with your local center.

Monthly Bonus Structure

Many Grifols locations offer escalating pay: first donation of the week pays less, second donation pays more. Donating twice weekly maximizes earnings.

Referral Program

Earn $20-$50 for each friend you refer who completes their first donation. Some locations offer higher referral bonuses during promotional periods.

Special Promotions

Grifols runs seasonal promotions, holiday bonuses, and random "power week" events with boosted pay. Follow your local center on social media for alerts.

How to Maximize Your Grifols Earnings

1

Donate Twice Weekly

Grifols typically pays more for the second donation of the week. Donating twice weekly (with at least one day between) maximizes both pay and bonuses.

2

Watch for Promotions

Follow Biomat USA on social media and check the app regularly. Grifols runs "power weeks" and holiday bonuses that can add $10-$30 per donation.

3

Stay Hydrated

Good hydration speeds up donation time and prevents deferrals. Drink 8-10 glasses of water the day before and morning of your donation.

4

Maintain Healthy Iron Levels

Eat iron-rich foods (red meat, spinach, beans) to avoid low hematocrit deferrals. Some donors take iron supplements between donations.

5

Use Referrals

Each successful referral earns you $20-$50. If you know others interested in donating, this can add significant income.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Grifols pay for plasma in 2026?
Grifols (Biomat USA) pays $30-$75 per donation depending on your weight and location. New donors can earn $700-$900 in their first month through special bonus programs. Regular donors typically earn $50-$65 per visit.
Is Biomat USA the same as Grifols?
Yes, Biomat USA is owned and operated by Grifols. Grifols also operates Talecris Plasma Resources centers. All three brands follow the same pay structure and donation requirements, though specific rates may vary by location.
Does Grifols pay more than CSL or BioLife?
Grifols base pay is competitive with other major centers, typically ranging $50-$75 per donation. New donor bonuses at Grifols ($700-$900) are similar to CSL and BioLife. The best-paying center depends on your specific location and current promotions.
How often can I donate plasma at Grifols?
You can donate plasma at Grifols up to twice per week (every 7 days), with at least one day between donations. This is the FDA maximum allowed for all plasma centers. Most donors donate 6-8 times per month.
What is the Grifols new donor bonus for 2026?
Grifols new donor bonuses for 2026 range from $700-$900 for your first 8 donations in the first month. Some locations offer up to $100 per visit for new donors during promotional periods. Check your local Biomat USA or Talecris for current offers.
How do I find a Grifols plasma center near me?
Visit grifolsplasma.com or download the Biomat USA app to find your nearest location. You can also search for "Biomat USA near me" or "Talecris Plasma near me" on Google Maps. Grifols has 300+ locations across the United States.
