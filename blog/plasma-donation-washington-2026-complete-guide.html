Washington Plasma Donation Tips

Weather Considerations

  • Rainy season (Oct-May): Plan for traffic delays
  • Winter: Mountain pass closures affect Eastern WA travel
  • Stay hydrated year-round: Pacific NW is humid but still drink water

Maximize Washington Earnings

  1. Start with Octapharma - $1,500 first-month bonus (Lynnwood location)
  2. Compare Seattle vs suburbs - Suburban centers often have shorter waits
  3. Use referral codes - Tech workers and students = lots of potential referrals
  4. No state income tax - Keep 100% of your plasma earnings
