💡 CSL Plasma Tax SECRETS (IRS Doesn't Advertise These!)

🎯 Secret #1: The $600 Magic Number

SHOCKING: CSL only sends 1099s if you earn $600+ in a year. Under $600? No tax reporting required by CSL!

Note: You're still technically required to report all income, but many small amounts go unreported.

💸 Secret #2: Standard Deduction Shield

2025 Protection: Standard deduction is $13,850 (single) / $27,700 (married). If plasma + other income is below this, you owe $0 federal tax!

🏥 Secret #3: Medical Expense Deductions

HIDDEN DEDUCTION: Travel to plasma centers, parking fees, and health supplements can be deductible medical expenses!

📊 Secret #4: Self-Employment vs. Other Income

CLASSIFICATION TRICK: Plasma income is usually "other income" (not self-employment), so no 15.3% self-employment tax!

🎓 Secret #5: Student Advantage

STUDENT HACK: Students with low income can earn up to $6,500 in plasma income and still qualify for maximum financial aid!

🧾 What CSL Sends You (Tax Documents)

📄 Form 1099-MISC

CSL sends this if you earned $600+ in a calendar year. Shows total payments in Box 3 (Other Income).

🗓️ Tax Calendar for Plasma Donors

January 31: Deadline for CSL to send 1099s

Quarterly: Make estimated payments if needed
