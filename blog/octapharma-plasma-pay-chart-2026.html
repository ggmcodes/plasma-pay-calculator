Frequently Asked Questions

Is Octapharma better than BioLife?

Octapharma and BioLife pay similar rates. BioLife often has slightly higher new donor bonuses, while Octapharma's OctaRewards program offers good returning donor value. Compare rates at your specific locations.

How does Octapharma pay you?

Octapharma loads payment onto a prepaid Visa debit card immediately after your donation. You can use it anywhere Visa is accepted or withdraw cash at ATMs.

How long does an Octapharma donation take?

First-time visits take 2-3 hours including screening. Return visits typically take 45-90 minutes.
