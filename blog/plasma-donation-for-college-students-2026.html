Student-Specific Tips

Maximize Your Earnings

  1. Start at semester beginning: Get new donor bonus early
  2. Refer roommates/friends: $50-100 per referral adds up fast
  3. Download the app: Surprise bonuses and coupons
  4. Donate consistently: Many centers reward streaks
  5. Check multiple centers: Compare new donor promos

Stay Healthy While Donating

  • Dining hall protein: Hit the protein station before donating
  • Dorm hydration: Keep a water bottle, refill constantly
  • Sleep enough: College sleep schedules affect donation
  • Limit alcohol: Party night = can't donate next day

Study During Donation

  • Bring flashcards: One-handed study method
  • Listen to lectures: Download recordings beforehand
  • Read textbooks: Tablet or phone holder helps
  • Watch recorded classes: Catch up on missed lectures

Common Student Concerns

"Will it affect my grades?"

No, if you prepare properly. Stay hydrated, eat well, and you'll feel fine. Many students say it's productive study time.

"Is it safe?"

Yes. Plasma donation is FDA-regulated and medically supervised. Millions of Americans donate safely each year.

"What about during finals?"

Perfect time! You're sitting in the library anyway - might as well get paid for similar sit-down time.
