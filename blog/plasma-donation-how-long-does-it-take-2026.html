Things That Speed Up Donation

  • Good hydration: Plasma flows faster
  • Appointments: Avoid walk-in waits
  • Off-peak hours: Weekday mornings often faster
  • Good veins: Experienced phlebotomists
  • Higher weight: More blood volume

Things That Slow Down Donation

  • Dehydration: Thick plasma = slow flow
  • Cold hands/arms: Slows blood flow
  • New staff: Learning process
  • Machine issues: Occasional technical delays
  • Health questions: Additional screening

Tips to Speed Up Your Visit

  1. Hydrate heavily: 16+ oz water before arrival
  2. Schedule appointment: Avoid walk-in waits
  3. Go at slow times: Weekday mornings, avoid lunch
  4. Eat protein: Helps plasma quality
  5. Keep arms warm: Wear long sleeves to center
  6. Complete app tasks: Answer questions beforehand
  7. Be consistent: Regular donors are faster
