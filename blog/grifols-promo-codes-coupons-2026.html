Grifols Promo Code FAQ

What's the difference between Grifols, Biomat, and Talecris?

They're all owned by the same company - Grifols. Biomat USA and Talecris are center brands that Grifols acquired. Same promotions and pay rates apply to all.

Is Grifols a good place to donate?

Yes. Grifols is one of the largest plasma companies globally. While their new donor bonus is slightly lower than Octapharma or BioLife, they're reliable and often have shorter wait times.

Does Grifols have an app?

Yes. Download the Grifols Plasma app to schedule appointments, track rewards, and find promotions.

Can I donate at Grifols if I've donated elsewhere?

Yes. Each plasma center chain maintains separate donor records. You may qualify for new donor bonuses at Grifols even if you've donated at other centers.
