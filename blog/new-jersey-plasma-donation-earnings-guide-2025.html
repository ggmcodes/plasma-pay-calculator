💡 New Jersey Optimization Strategies

1. Tax Minimization Tactics

✅ NJ Tax Optimization

  • Maximize deductions: Track mileage, parking, supplements
  • Business expense strategy: Treat as self-employment if significant income
  • Timing strategy: Spread income across tax years when possible
  • Professional help: CPA consultation pays for itself with NJ's complexity

2. Location Arbitrage

  • North vs South NJ: Northern centers pay 15-20% more
  • University timing: Higher rates during academic year

  • Transportation costs: NJ tolls and gas prices are above national average

🎯 NJ Bonus Strategy

  • Holiday bonuses: Thanksgiving-Christmas period offers premiums
