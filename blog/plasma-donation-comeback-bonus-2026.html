What Are Comeback Bonuses?

Comeback bonuses (also called "lapsed donor" or "return donor" bonuses) are extra payments offered to donors who haven't donated for a while. Centers want to re-activate dormant donors.

Why Centers Offer These

  • Cheaper than new donors: Already in system, no full screening needed
  • Proven donors: You've successfully donated before
  • Database reactivation: Centers want to maintain active donor pool
  • Seasonal needs: May increase during high-demand periods

  • Health screening: May need updated physical exam

  • Health questionnaire: Updated medical history
  • Possible delays: Return visit may take longer than regular

Maximizing Comeback Bonuses

Strategic Approach

Some donors intentionally take breaks to maximize earnings:

  1. Donate regularly: Build up donation history
  2. Take a break: 3-6 months off
  3. Watch for offers: Centers often reach out with bonuses
  4. Return for bonus: Collect comeback bonus
  5. Donate regularly again: Then consider another break later

Is This Worth It?

Do the math:

  • 3 months regular donating: ~$2,400 (at $800/month)
  • 3 months off + comeback: $0 + ~$300 bonus
  • Net loss: ~$2,100 by taking time off

Verdict: Usually NOT worth taking extended breaks just for comeback bonuses. But if you're taking a break anyway (vacation, health, busy period), the comeback bonus is a nice incentive to return.

Pro Tips for Comeback Bonuses

  1. Keep contact info updated: Centers email/text offers to active contacts
  2. Check apps regularly: Even when not donating actively
  3. Ask at centers: Staff know about current comeback promotions
  4. Compare centers: If you've donated at multiple centers, compare offers
  5. Time it right: January and holiday seasons often have better offers

When Comeback Bonuses Make Sense

  • Natural breaks: After vacation, illness, or busy period
  • Life changes: Returning after move, job change, etc.
  • Seasonal donating: If you only donate certain times of year
  • Testing new center: Try a new center's comeback offer
