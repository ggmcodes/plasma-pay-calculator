How Referral Bonuses Work

Most plasma centers offer bonuses when you refer friends who become donors. Here's the typical process:

Standard Referral Process

  1. Get your referral code: Find in app or ask staff
  2. Share with friend: Give them your code before their first visit
  3. Friend signs up: Uses your code at registration
  4. Friend donates: Must complete required donations
  5. You get paid: Bonus added to your account

Maximizing Referral Earnings

Who to Refer

  • Friends/family: Obvious first choice
  • Coworkers: People looking for side income
  • College classmates: Students often need money
  • Neighbors: Local community members
  • Social media: Share (carefully) on social platforms

How to Pitch It

When talking to potential referrals, emphasize:

  • The money: "$500+ first month" gets attention
  • Helping others: Plasma saves lives
  • Easy process: "Sit and scroll your phone for an hour"
  • Your experience: Share your positive experience

Referral Math

If you refer 5 friends who complete their donations:

  • 5 referrals x $75 each = $375
  • That's almost a week of donations from referrals alone

Important Referral Rules

Common Requirements

  • New donors only: Can't refer existing donors
  • Must use code: Friend must enter code at sign-up
  • Must complete donations: Usually 2-4 visits required
  • Time limits: Friend may need to complete within 30-60 days
  • Active account: You must be an active donor

What Disqualifies a Referral

  • Friend was already in the center's system
  • Friend doesn't complete required donations
  • Code wasn't entered at registration
  • You're no longer an active donor
  • Fraudulent referrals

Referral Bonus Tips

  1. Share code BEFORE they go: Must be entered at registration
  2. Follow up: Encourage friend to complete required donations
  3. Track your referrals: Most apps show referral status
  4. Stack with promotions: Refer during high-bonus periods
  5. Be honest: Don't oversell - happy referrals donate long-term
