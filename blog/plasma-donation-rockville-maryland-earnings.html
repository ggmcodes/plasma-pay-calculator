‚ùì Rockville Plasma Donation FAQ

Do DC area plasma centers pay more than other regions?

Yes, the DC metro area typically pays 20-35% more than national averages due to high cost of living and competitive job market.

Can federal employees donate plasma?

Yes, there are no restrictions on federal employees donating plasma. Many centers near DC are familiar with government schedules.

Is it worth taking Metro to donate plasma?

Yes, even after Metro costs ($6.75 round trip), net earnings of $63-93 per visit make it worthwhile for many donors.

Do any centers offer federal employee discounts or bonuses?

While not official, some centers in the DC area occasionally run promotions targeting government workers, especially during pay periods.

Rockville consistently ranks in the top 10% nationally for plasma compensation, comparable to areas like Palo Alto, CA or Greenwich, CT.
