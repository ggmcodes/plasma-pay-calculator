Quick Answer

Payment typically loads to your prepaid card within minutes of completing donation. You can access funds immediately via ATM or purchases. Bonuses may load same day or after meeting requirements. Bank transfers take 1-3 business days.

Ways to Access Your Money

Immediate Access

  • ATM withdrawal: Cash available immediately
  • Debit purchases: Use card anywhere Visa/Mastercard accepted
  • Cash back: At grocery stores, gas stations
  • Online purchases: Use card number

ATM Considerations

Minimizing ATM Fees

  • Use partner network ATMs (check app for locations)
  • Get cash back at stores instead
  • Withdraw larger amounts less frequently
  • Check card terms for fee-free withdrawals

Handling Payment Issues

If Payment Is Missing

  1. Check card balance in app
  2. Verify donation was marked complete

  4. Contact center staff with donation date/time

If Amount Is Wrong

  • Check promotion requirements

Frequently Asked Questions

When do you get paid for plasma donation?

Most centers load payment to your prepaid card within minutes of completing donation. You can typically access funds immediately at ATMs or for purchases. Some centers may process within 24 hours.

How is plasma donation money paid?

Payment is loaded onto a center-provided prepaid debit card (CSL, BioLife, etc.). You can use it like any debit card for purchases, ATM withdrawals, or transfer to your bank account.

When do plasma bonuses get paid?

Bonus payments vary: visit bonuses load same day, monthly bonuses after reaching goal, referral bonuses after friend completes requirements, and new donor completion bonuses after final qualifying visit.

Can I get plasma payment as cash?

You can withdraw cash from your prepaid card at ATMs. Some centers have partner ATMs without fees. Direct cash payments at the center are not typically available.

How do I transfer plasma money to my bank account?

Most plasma cards allow bank transfers through the card's app or website. Transfers typically take 1-3 business days. Some cards may charge transfer fees.

What if my plasma payment is missing or wrong?

Check your card balance through the app first. If payment is missing, contact center staff within 24-48 hours with your donation confirmation. Most payment issues resolve quickly.
