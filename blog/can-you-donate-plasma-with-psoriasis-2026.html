Quick Answer

IT DEPENDS

Severity and medications matter

Can You Donate Plasma With Psoriasis?

Eligibility depends on your medications and severity in 2026:

  • Mild psoriasis: Often can donate
  • Topical treatments only: Usually OK
  • Biologics: Usually disqualify
  • Needle site clear: Arms must be free of lesions

Needle Site Considerations

Your arms must be suitable for venipuncture:

  • Clear inner arms: No lesions at needle site
  • No open sores: Breaks in skin disqualify
  • Infection risk: Plaques near veins are concerning
  • Other areas OK: Psoriasis elsewhere doesn't matter

Why Biologics Disqualify

Biologic medications affect immunity:

  • Suppress immune system components
  • May affect plasma quality
  • Medication present in plasma
  • Recipient safety concerns

Tips for Psoriasis Donors

  1. Keep arms clear: Treat arms aggressively to keep needle site clear
  2. Disclose condition: Be honest during screening
  3. List medications: Know exactly what you're taking
  4. Moisturize: Healthy skin = better venous access
  5. Avoid flares: Don't donate during active flares

Frequently Asked Questions

I have a small patch on my elbow only - can I donate?

Probably yes, if you're only using topical treatments. A small patch away from the inner arm/needle site shouldn't disqualify you. Be honest during screening.

I use Humira for my psoriasis - can I donate?

No. Humira (adalimumab) and other biologics typically disqualify you from plasma donation because they suppress the immune system.

My psoriasis is in remission and I'm off all medications - can I donate?

If you're off biologics and immunosuppressants for the required waiting period and only have mild symptoms controlled topically, you may be eligible. Check with your specific center.

I have psoriatic arthritis but my skin is clear - does that matter?

Psoriatic arthritis usually requires systemic medications (methotrexate, biologics) that disqualify donation. The skin being clear doesn't change the medication issue.

Summary

Mild psoriasis with topical treatments usually allows donation. Moderate-to-severe psoriasis requiring biologics or systemic medications typically disqualifies.
