6 Which Should You Choose?

The Best Strategy

Rather than picking one and sticking with it forever, consider this approach:

  1. 1

    Start with the best new donor bonus

    Compare current promotions at both centers in your area. New donor rates are where the real money is.

  2. 2

    Complete the new donor program

    Finish all 8 donations to maximize that initial bonus. Don't switch early.

  3. 3

    Evaluate regular rates

    After the new donor period, compare ongoing pay rates and consider switching if another center pays more.

  4. 4

    Watch for comeback bonuses

    After 6+ months away, you may qualify for "returning donor" bonuses at your original center.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Note

You can only donate at one center at a time due to the national donor database (NDDR). Attempting to donate at multiple centers simultaneously will result in deferral and potential banning from all centers. When switching, you must wait for your records to transfer (usually automatic) or inform the new center about your previous donation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch from Grifols to CSL (or vice versa)? ‚ñº
Yes, you can switch centers. The national database tracks your donations regardless of company. When you visit a new center, they'll see your donation history and determine when you're eligible. You typically need to wait 48+ hours since your last donation anywhere.
Will I get new donor bonuses at the second company? ‚ñº
No. "New donor" means new to plasma donation, not new to that company. If you've donated plasma anywhere before, you're considered a returning donor. However, some centers offer "returning donor" bonuses if you haven't donated in 6+ months.
Is the plasma donation process the same at both? ‚ñº
The actual plasma collection process is virtually identical. Both use similar apheresis machines. The differences are in facilities, staff, wait times, and payment structures‚Äînot the medical procedure itself.
Which company has better customer service? ‚ñº
This varies dramatically by individual center. Both companies have locations with excellent staff and locations with problems. Read Google reviews for the specific centers near you rather than relying on company-wide reputation.
