Frequently Asked Questions

Does BioLife or CSL Plasma pay more?

Pay varies by location and time. Both offer similar base rates ($20-50 per visit). New donor bonuses can differ significantly. Check current promotions at your local centers as they change frequently.

Which has better new donor bonuses - BioLife or CSL?

Both offer competitive new donor promotions, typically $700-900+ in the first month. BioLife often advertises total bonus amounts while CSL shows per-visit rates. Compare local offers directly.

Is BioLife or CSL faster?

Wait times vary by location, day, and time. Both centers have appointments, but walk-ins may wait longer. Check reviews for your specific location. Using the center's app can reduce wait times.

Can I switch from BioLife to CSL or vice versa?

Yes, you can switch centers. You'll need to complete new donor registration at the new center. Your 48-hour waiting period from your last donation still applies across all centers.

Which plasma center has more locations?

CSL Plasma has more locations nationwide (350+) compared to BioLife (170+). However, BioLife may have more centers in certain regions. Check availability in your area.

Do BioLife and CSL have the same eligibility requirements?

Requirements are similar as they follow FDA guidelines. Both require minimum age (18), weight (110 lbs), valid ID, and passing health screening. Minor policy differences may exist.
