Quick Answer

CSL Plasma returning donors earn $50-$100 per visit in 2026, depending on weight, location, and active promotions. Donating twice weekly yields $400-$900/month. The second donation each week typically pays $10-$20 more than the first.

CSL Plasma Returning Donor Overview

After completing your new donor bonus period at CSL Plasma, you transition to the returning donor pay schedule. While individual donation pay is lower than new donor rates, consistent twice-weekly donations still generate significant monthly income. Understanding the returning donor pay structure helps you maximize long-term earnings.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does CSL Plasma pay returning donors?

CSL Plasma returning donors earn $50-$100 per visit depending on weight and promotions. Monthly earnings range from $400-$700 for twice-weekly donors, with opportunities to earn more through frequency bonuses and promotional events.

Do returning donors get bonuses at CSL Plasma?

Yes, CSL Plasma offers multiple bonus opportunities for returning donors including monthly frequency bonuses ($20-$50), seasonal promotions, referral bonuses ($50-$100), and loyalty rewards programs.

Can I get the new donor bonus again at CSL Plasma?

After 6+ months of inactivity, some centers may offer returning donors a "lapsed donor" bonus that's similar to (but usually lower than) the new donor bonus. Check with your local CSL Plasma for current re-enrollment offers.
