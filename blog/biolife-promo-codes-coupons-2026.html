How to Get Your Referral Code

  1. Open the BioLife app
  2. Go to "Refer a Friend" section
  3. Copy your unique referral code
  4. Share with friends via text, email, or social media
Referral Requirements

To earn referral bonuses: You must have donated within the past 6 months. Your friend must be a new BioLife donor (never donated at any BioLife location). Your friend must complete at least one successful donation.

BioLife Promo Code FAQ

Do BioLife promo codes really work?

Yes, but they're location-specific and change frequently. Always verify with your local center before relying on a code found online.

Can I use multiple promo codes?

Generally no - only one promo code per new donor signup. However, referral bonuses can stack with new donor bonuses.

What if my promo code doesn't work?

Ask the center staff. Sometimes codes are region-specific or have expired. They may have a current local promotion that works instead.

How do I get the $1,200 bonus?

Complete 8 donations within your first 30 days as a new donor. The bonus is paid incrementally with the largest amount on your 8th donation.

Can returning donors use promo codes?

If you haven't donated at BioLife in 6+ months, you may qualify for "lapsed donor" promotions. Check with your local center.
