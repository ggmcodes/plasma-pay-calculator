Frequently Asked Questions

How much does ABO Plasma pay per donation?

ABO Plasma pays $40-$60 per donation for repeat donors, depending on weight, location, and active promotions. New donors earn significantly more through first-month bonus programs of $500-$800.

What is the ABO Plasma new donor bonus?

New donors at ABO Plasma can earn $500-$800 in their first month by completing 6-8 donations within the bonus period. Exact amounts vary by location and current promotions.

Does ABO Plasma pay more for heavier donors?

Yes, donors weighing 175+ lbs earn more because they can safely donate larger plasma volumes (880 mL vs 690 mL for lighter donors). The difference is typically $10-$20 more per donation.

How does ABO Plasma pay you?

ABO Plasma pays via prepaid debit card loaded immediately after each donation. You can use it for purchases, ATM withdrawals, or transfer funds to your bank account.

How often can you donate at ABO Plasma?

You can donate up to twice per week with at least 2 days (48 hours) between donations. Most donors visit on a Monday/Thursday or Tuesday/Friday schedule to maximize monthly earnings.
