Texas Plasma Donation Tips

Beat the Texas Heat

  • Stay extra hydrated - Texas heat causes faster dehydration
  • Donate in the morning - Cooler and less crowded
  • Park in shade - Your car will be cooler when you finish
  • Bring a cold water bottle - Many centers allow drinks

Maximize Texas Earnings

  1. Start with Octapharma - Highest first-month bonus ($1,500)
  2. Switch to BioLife - After new donor period, best ongoing rates
  3. Use referral codes - Earn $50-$100 per friend
  4. Check city-specific promos - Houston/Dallas often have extra bonuses
  5. Donate consistently - Loyalty bonuses add up
