Michigan Plasma Donation Tips

Winter Donation Tips

  • Stay hydrated indoors: Heating dries you out
  • Warm up before: Cold veins are harder to access
  • Wear layers: Easy to adjust for cold centers
  • Watch weather: Centers may close in severe winter storms
