Tips to Maximize CSL Weight-Based Pay

1

Weigh In at the Right Time

Your body weight fluctuates 2-5 lbs throughout the day. Weigh-ins in the afternoon after eating and drinking typically show higher weights.

2

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water before your appointment. A well-hydrated body weighs more, and hydration speeds up your donation.

3

If You're Close to a Threshold

If you weigh 148 lbs or 173 lbs, gaining just a couple pounds could bump you to the next pay tier - worth $50-$100+ more per month!

Frequently Asked Questions

Does CSL weigh you at every visit?

Yes, CSL weighs you at every donation to determine your payment tier and ensure safe plasma collection volumes.

What if my weight changes between visits?

Your pay adjusts based on your weight at each visit. If you weigh more one week, you'll earn more. Weight fluctuations are normal.

Is there a maximum weight for CSL Plasma?

There's no official maximum weight, but donors must be able to sit comfortably in donation chairs. Most centers accommodate donors up to 400+ lbs.
