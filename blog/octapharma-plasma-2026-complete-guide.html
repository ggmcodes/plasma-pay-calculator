Octapharma FAQ

How long does an Octapharma donation take?

First visit: 2-3 hours (includes physical, paperwork). Return visits: 45-90 minutes depending on your flow rate and wait times.

Can I donate at Octapharma and another center?

No. All plasma centers use a national database to track donations. You can only donate at one center at a time, and must wait the required 48 hours between donations.

How do I get the $1,500 bonus?

Complete 8 donations within your first 30 days as a new donor. The bonus is paid incrementally with each donation.

What if I miss a donation in my first month?

You may not qualify for the full bonus if you don't complete 8 donations in 30 days. Contact your local center for specific policies.
