Quick Answer

ADMA BioCenters pays $50-$75 per donation for repeat donors, with total monthly earnings of $400-$1,000 when donating twice weekly. New donors can earn $700-$1,200 in their first month through promotional bonuses. Pay varies by weight (higher weight = more pay), location, and current promotions. ADMA BioCenters Overview

ADMA BioCenters operates a growing network of plasma donation centers across the United States, focusing on collecting high-quality plasma for manufacturing life-saving medications. Unlike some larger chains, ADMA BioCenters emphasizes personalized donor care and streamlined donation experiences.

Founded as part of ADMA Biologics Inc., a biopharmaceutical company specializing in plasma-derived therapeutics, ADMA BioCenters has expanded strategically to serve communities while maintaining strict quality standards. The company collects plasma used in treatments for primary immunodeficiency diseases, alpha-1 antitrypsin deficiency, and other serious conditions.

Key Facts About ADMA BioCenters

  • Parent Company: ADMA Biologics Inc. (publicly traded: ADMA)
  • Number of Centers: 15+ locations across multiple states
