Eligibility FAQ

What if I'm not sure about my eligibility?

Call the plasma center before your visit. Explain your situation and they can often tell you if you're likely to qualify, saving you a trip if there's an issue.

Can I donate if I have a cold?

No. Wait until you're fully recovered - no symptoms for at least 24-48 hours.

Does donating plasma show up on drug tests?

No. Plasma donation itself doesn't affect drug tests. However, centers may test your blood for certain substances.

Can I donate while pregnant or breastfeeding?

No. Wait 6 weeks after delivery and consult with the center about breastfeeding policies.
