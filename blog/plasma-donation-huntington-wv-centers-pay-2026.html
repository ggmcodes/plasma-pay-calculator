Huntington FAQs

Which plasma center pays the most in Huntington WV?
CSL Plasma is the primary high-paying option in Huntington with bonuses up to $900. BioLife in Charleston (45 min away) offers up to $1,000 for new donors.
Can I donate plasma at multiple centers in the Tri-State area?
No, you can only be registered at one plasma center at a time. However, you can transfer between centers of the same company if needed.
