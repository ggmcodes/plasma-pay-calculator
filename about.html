About Sunset Study Club

We're reading. We're writing. We're showing up. Creating a soft, focused space for mindful productivity.

Our Story

Sunset Study Club began with a simple belief: productivity doesn't have to be stressful. In a world of hustle culture and constant notifications, we create peaceful spaces where deep work can flourish.

Through ambient guitar music, soft ASMR textures, and mindful study sessions, we've built a community that values presence over pressure. Every video, every tool recommendation, every moment of our content is designed to help you show up consistently without burning out.

Whether you're journaling, reading, planning, or diving into focused work, our sessions provide the gentle structure and calming atmosphere you need to do your best thinking.

What You'll Find Here

Study Sessions: Extended Pomodoro blocks with ambient guitar, gentle ASMR, and peaceful visuals. Perfect for reading, writing, coding, or any focused work.

Mindful Tools: Carefully chosen study essentials that enhance your practice. From quality pens to meditation timers, every recommendation supports mindful productivity.

Community: A growing group of people who believe in showing up consistently rather than burning out. We're here to support each other's learning journey.

Original Music: All ambient guitar tracks are composed and recorded specifically for our study sessions. Each piece is designed to enhance focus without becoming distracting.
